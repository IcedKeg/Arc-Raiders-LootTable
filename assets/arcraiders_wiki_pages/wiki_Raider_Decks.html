The Raider Deck is like a battle pass, where you can spend Cred in order to unlock cosmetic rewards. There are a number of rewards to unlock per page in order to progress to the next page. The number of pages in each Raider Deck may vary. At launch, the Raider Decks will be free, but you can expect a mix of paid and free Raider Decks as new ones are added.

*Some Raider Decks will be permanent and others will be available for a limited time.

Raider Decks

Raider Deck AR/RD 001

Page 1


  • MAYHEM PACK: x3 Recorder, x5 Noisemaker, x5 Remote Raider Flare, x3 Smoke Grenade, x3 Blue Light Stick, x3 Red Light Stick, x3 Green Light Stick
  • SCOUT PACK: x3 Binoculars, x5 Tagging Grenade, x3 Zipline, x5 Yellow Light Stick

Page 2

Spend 290 cred to unlock

  • ARC HUNTER PACK: Tactical Mk. 2, x3 Lure Grenade, x3 Wolfpack
  • IMPROVISATION PACK: x3 Flame Spray, x3 Blaze Grenade Trap, x3 Gas Grenade Trap, x5 Herbal Bandage

Page 3

Spend 480 cred to unlock

  • PROTECTION PACK: x2 Looting Mk. 2, x5 Door Blocker, x5 Noisemaker, x3 Explosive Mine, x5 Jolt Mine
  • NINJA PACK: Snap Hook, x5 Li'l Smoke Grenade

Page 4

  • MEDIC PACK: Tactical Mk. 3 (Healing), x3 Defibrillator, x3 Vita Spray, x3 Surge Shield Recharger

Page 5

  • KEYHOLDER PACK: x2 Looting Mk. 3 (Cautious), x2 Raider Hatch Key, Buried City Town Hall Key, Dam Staff Room Key, Spaceport Container Storage Key

Page 6
