“Unlike many here, this isn’t Apollo’s first settlement. He claims to have lived in several, although he is a bit vague on the details. That said, his energy for improvement and his constant cheer do have their uses.” - Shani

Apollo runs a store by Speranza’s Piazza, selling primarily makeshift grenades and gadgets. Most of his wares are of his own design, and though they may seem rudimentary to the untrained eye, they are thoroughly filed-tested by the most daring Raiders.
