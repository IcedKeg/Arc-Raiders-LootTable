Mastery challenges are hard challenges for Raiders to complete.

Each mastery has a different ending reward recipe and focuses on a different aspect of ARC Raiders gameplay. You must extract safely for mastery progress to be counted.

Mastery points are not cumulative, the high score from a successful raid is counted but is not added to a running total.
