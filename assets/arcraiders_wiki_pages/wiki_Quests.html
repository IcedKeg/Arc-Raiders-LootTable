Quests are a key method of progression, offering Raider Coins, XP, and various items as rewards. Each quest has specific objectives, and some provide gear upon acceptance to aid in completing the task.

Quests

Quest Trader Required Location Objective Reward
A Bad Feeling Celeste Any
  • Find and search any ARC Probe or ARC Courier
  • 10x Metal Parts
  • 5x Steel Spring
  • 5x Duct Tape
A Balanced Harvest Celeste Dam Battlegrounds In One Round
  • Go to the Research & Administration building
  • Locate Lab 1 on the upper floor above the reception
  • Search for any traces of the agricultural research project
  • 3x Advanced Mechanical Components
  • 3x Medium Gun Parts
  • 10x Steel Spring
A Better Use Tian Wen Any
  • Request in a Supply Drop from a Call Station
  • Loot a Supply Drop
  • Extended Light Magazine I
  • Compensator I
  • Stable Stock I
A First Foothold Apollo The Blue Gate
  • Nail down the roof plates on the Raider structure near Trapper's Glade
  • Stabilize the observation deck near the Ridgeline
  • Enable the comms terminal near the Olive Grove
  • Rotate the satellite dishes on the church roof, north of the Data Vault
  • x3 Shrapnel Grenade
  • x3 Snap Blast Grenade
  • x3 Heavy Fuze Grenade
A Lay Of The Land Shani Spaceport
  • Reach the Jiangsu Warehouse
  • Find the shipping notes in the foreman's office
  • Locate the scanners on the upper floor of Control Tower A6
  • Deliver 1 LiDAR Scanners to Shani
  • Dam Testing Annex Key
  • 3x Zipline
  • 2x Smoke Grenade

  • Tactical Mk. 3 (Healing)
  • 1x Surge Shield Recharger
A Symbol Of Unification Celeste Dam Battlegrounds In One Round

  • 5x Duct Tape
A Warm Place To Rest Apollo Buried City

After Rain Comes Celeste Buried City

  • 3x Antiseptic
  • 5x Durable Cloth

  • Find and unlock the rear door of an armored patrol car

  • 3x Defibrillator

  • On Spaceport, mark the South Trench Tower

  • Stable Stock III
  • 80x Medium Ammo
