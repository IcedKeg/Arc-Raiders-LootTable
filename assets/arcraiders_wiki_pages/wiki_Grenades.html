Grenades are throwable devices used to damage Raiders and ARCs or for non-damaging utility purposes (e.g., to create a smoke screen, distract Arcs, or illuminate an area. In a pinch, certain ARC parts or scavenged loot can be thrown and used as makeshift grenades.

Grenades

Name Image Description
Blaze Grenade A grenade that detonates on impact, covering an area in fire that deals damage over time.
Gas Grenade A grenade that creates a lingering toxic cloud on impact, draining the stamina of any Raiders within its area of effect.
Heavy Fuze Grenade A grenade that detonates after a delay, dealing explosive damage in its radius.
Li'l Smoke Grenade A Grenade that pops into a thick but small smoke cloud on impact, blocking visibility from ARC and other Raiders.
Light Impact Grenade A grenade that detonates on impact, dealing explosive damage in a small radius.
Light Stick A throwable chemical light that illuminates the area around it.
Lure Grenade A noisy device that sticks to surfaces distracting nearby ARC machines and drawing their fire.
Showstopper A grenade that detonates after a delay, stunning enemies within it's radius.
Shrapnel Grenade A makeshift fuze grenade that bursts into razor-sharp fragments upon detonation.
Smoke Grenade A grenade that creates a lingering smoke cloud on impact, blocking visibility from other Raiders.
Snap Blast Grenade A grenade that sticks to surfaces, dealing explosive damage after a short delay.
Tagging Grenade A grenade that detonates after a delay, tagging Raiders and ARC enemies in an area, allowing you to briefly track their location.
Trigger 'Nade A remote-detonated grenade that can stick to surfaces and ARC, dealing explosive damage when triggered.
Wolfpack A grenade that scatter into multiple homing missiles, each one targeting ARC and dealing explosive damage on impact.

Name Image Must Be Shot to Detonate Description
Crude Explosives No Used to craft explosives. Can be recycled into crafting materials.
Fireball Burner No Can be recycled into ARC Alloy.
Hornet Driver No Can be recycled into ARC Alloy.
Leaper Pulse Unit No Can be recycled into crafting materials.
Rubber Duck No Always there to lend an ear, should you need it.
Snitch Scanner No Can be recycled into ARC Alloy.
Synthesized Fuel Yes Used to craft utility items and explosives. Can be recycled into chemicals.
