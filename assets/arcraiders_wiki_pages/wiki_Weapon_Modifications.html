Weapon Modifications are items you can throw onto your weapons while Topside or down in Speranza that provide upgrades to you weapons.

Craft them at the Gunsmith in your workshop, find them in raid, or buy some basic modifications from Tian Wen.

Check each weapon's individual page to see which modification slots are available and compatible.
