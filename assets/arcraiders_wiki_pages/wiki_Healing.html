Healing items are used by all Raiders to survive while Topside. Some healing items focus on health, some recharging shield, and others stamina.

Health

Name Image Healing Duration Description
Agave 1/s 10s A piece of an agave leaf. Can be used to regain a small amount of health.
Bandage 2/s 10s A medical item that gradually restores health over time.
Defibrillator 50 - An injection to quickly revive downed raiders and restores some health.
Expired Pasta 15 - Can (reluctantly) be consumed to restore small amounts of health.
Fabric 0.4/s 25s Can be used to slowly restore small amounts of health.
Fruit Mix 25 - A food item that moderately increases both health and stamina.
Herbal Bandage 3.5/s 10s An improvised medical item that gradually restores health over time.
Moss 1/s 10s Can be consumed to regain a small amount of health.
Mushroom 15 - Can be consumed to regain a small amount of health.
Resin 1/s 10s Can be used to gradually restore a small amount of health over time.
Sterilized Bandage 5/s 10s A medical item that gradually restores a large amount of health over time.
Vita Shot 50 - A medical item that restores a large amount of health.
Vita Spray 10/s 150 Capacity A medical item that continuously restores health during use. Can be used on yourself or your allies.

Shield

Name Image Recharge Duration Description
ARC Powercell 2/s 10s A valuable resource that drops from all ARC enemies.
Integrated Shield Recharger ? ? ?
Shield Recharger 4/s 10s A handheld repair kit that recharges a shield over time.
Surge Shield Recharger 50 - A handheld kit that recharges a shield on use.

Stamina

Name Image Stamina Duration Description
Adrenaline Shot 5/s + 100 10s A serum that fully restores stamina and temporarily increases stamina regeneration.
Agave Juice 5/s 10s A concoction that temporarily increases stamina regeneration, at a small initial cost to health.
Apricot 20 - A sun ripe apricot. Can be consumed for a small amount of stamina.
Bloated Tuna Can 25 - Something tells you that you don't want to open this...
Fruit Mix 50 - A food item that moderately increases both health and stamina.
Lemon 20 - Can be consumed for a small amount of stamina.
Olives 20 - Can be consumed for a small amount of stamina.
Prickly Pear 20 - Can be consumed for a small amount of stamina.
