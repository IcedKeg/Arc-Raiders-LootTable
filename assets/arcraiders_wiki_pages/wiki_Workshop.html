The workshop lets you craft gear and items. You start with a free basic bench, which enables you to craft basic weapons and most types of ammo. Each workbench requires different materials to build and upgrade, with a maximum level of 3.

Scrappy

This rooster has been a resident of the workshop since the day you moved in, and likely long before that. Will periodically bring back dubiously-sourced materials and share them with you.

Scrappy brings you metal parts, fabric, plastic parts, chemicals, rubber parts, and assorted seeds.

Blueprints

Blueprints are a core component of the Workshop system. They are used to craft specific items such as weapons, materials, and gadgets.

Blueprints are most reliably found in the loot containers of locked rooms, but can occasionally be rewards from quests.

A blueprint must be extracted before it can be used. Place it in your Safe Pocket to guarantee you'll be able to learn it. Once returned to Speranza, consuming the blueprint will add it to the relevant workshop station.
