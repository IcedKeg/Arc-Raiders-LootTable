When you get a Blueprint out you will need to select it then choose "Learn and Consume" to gain that blueprint. All blueprints can be sold for 5,000 Coins. All Blueprints in the scavenging loot pool are able to be unlocked as a trial reward.

This is excluding traveling tinkerer skill crafts!
