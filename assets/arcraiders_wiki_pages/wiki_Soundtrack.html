This page contains information about the Official Soundtrack (OST) for ARC Raiders.

ARC Raiders Soundtrack

ARC Raiders Original Video Game Soundtrack

The Original Soundtrack of the game, released 8 days after the Release, ARC Raiders (Original Video Game Soundtrack), includes 24 tracks. It was composed by Embark, as well as Patrik Andrén and Johan Söderqvist, both known for their work on the Battlefield series. The soundtrack blends retro-futuristic textures, gritty basslines and modular synths suitable for the game’s sci-fi setting.

Themes from ARC Raiders (Original Video Game Soundtrack)
